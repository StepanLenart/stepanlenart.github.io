Chris Bumsted in his prime

Christopher Adam Bumstead is a Canadian professional IFBB bodybuilder. He was born February 2. 1995 Ottawa-Canada

Bumstead is the reigning winner of the Mr. Olympia Classic Physique he won the competition 4 times alredy and that in:

  • 2019
  • 2020
  • 2021
  • 2022
He was also runner-up in 2017 and 2018. He is often referred to by his nickname Cbum. Bumstead maintains a large online presence with content focusing on his lifestyle and bodybuilding.
